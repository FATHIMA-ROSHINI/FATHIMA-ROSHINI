## 🚀 Projects

### 🚁 AirGuardian — Indoor Air Quality Mapping Drone *(Hardware + AI)*

> An autonomous **indoor drone** that performs real-time air quality mapping in industrial and enclosed environments.

AirGuardian is a hardware-software integrated system combining autonomous drone navigation with AI-driven spatial analysis to detect air quality variations, hazardous gas presence, and environmental anomalies across indoor spaces. The drone navigates environments autonomously, building dynamic air quality maps to enable proactive safety responses.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Real-time hazardous gas detection and 3D spatial air quality mapping
- AI-driven environmental anomaly identification
- Autonomous indoor navigation and coverage planning
- Smart multi-sensor data collection and analysis

`Hardware` `Autonomous Navigation` `AI Monitoring` `Environmental Intelligence` `Smart Sensing` `Drone Systems`

---

### 🫁 Respi-AI — Respiratory Risk Assessment System *(Software)*

> An AI-powered system for **early respiratory risk assessment** using cough and breathing sound analysis.

Respi-AI leverages **deep learning and audio signal processing** to identify potential respiratory abnormalities from cough patterns and breathing sounds — enabling accessible, non-invasive, and privacy-aware preliminary screening. Particularly valuable for **telemedicine and rural healthcare** contexts where clinical access is limited.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Deep learning–based cough and breathing audio classification
- Early-stage respiratory anomaly pattern detection
- Non-invasive, privacy-aware screening pipeline
- Designed for telemedicine and low-resource healthcare settings

> ⚠️ *Respi-AI is not a medical diagnostic tool. It is designed for risk assessment and early indication only — not clinical diagnosis.*

`Deep Learning` `Audio Signal Processing` `Healthcare AI` `Telemedicine` `Privacy-Aware AI`

---

### 👁️ NeuroSight — On-Device Eye Movement AI Screening *(Full-Stack Web App)*

> A **browser-based AI screening system** that processes eye-movement data entirely on-device for maximum privacy.

NeuroSight is a full-stack intelligent vision system built with a **React frontend** and **FastAPI backend**, implementing real-time **TensorFlow.js inference** directly in the browser — eliminating the need to transmit sensitive health data to any external server.

**Key Capabilities:**
- On-device TensorFlow.js inference with zero data transmission
- Real-time eye-movement tracking, processing, and risk assessment scoring
- Live metrics dashboard with interactive visualization
- Glassmorphism UI design and Progressive Web App (PWA) capabilities

`React` `FastAPI` `TensorFlow.js` `Computer Vision` `PWA` `On-Device AI` `Glassmorphism UI`
