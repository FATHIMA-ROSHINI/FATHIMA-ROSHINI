AIR GUARDIAN DRONE

Problem

Traditional manual drones struggle with stable indoor flight due to GPS denial and sensor noise.

Solution

Built an autonomous micro-drone with optical flow stabilization, positional control using INAV, and predictive drift correction algorithms.

Tech Stack

Python INAV Pixhawk Optical Flow Kalman Filters

Outcome

Achieved ~40% improvement in indoor stability and reduced drift to <25cm over a 3m hover duration.
